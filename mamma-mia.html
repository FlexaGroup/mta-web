Mamma Mia

Join the fun at MAMMA MIA!, the world's sunniest, most exhilarating smash-hit musical! Since premiering in London's West
End over 24 years ago, “the ultimate feel-good show” (Classic FM) has thrilled over 65 million people around the world.

Fall in love with the irresistibly funny tale of a mother, a daughter and three possible dads on a Greek island idyll, all
unfolding to the magic of ABBA's timeless songs. The worldwide phenomenon has now been turned into two record-breaking
movies - MAMMA MIA! The Movie and MAMMA MIA! Here We Go Again.

Whatever age you are, you can't help but have the time of your life at MAMMA MIA!
